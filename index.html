About

I'm a graphic designer who believes design and strategy should sit at the same table.

I'm majoring in Digital Communication Media & Multimedia, and I care about the same question at every scale — what makes someone stop and look. Sometimes that's a poster in a hallway, sometimes it's a caption in a feed. I'm building on that through Adobe's Digital Marketing and Social Media Content & Strategy specialization, an Adobe Digital Marketing Strategy specialization, and Meta's Social Media Marketing program, alongside coursework in visual storytelling and multimedia.

Outside of class, I design flyers, ads, and campaigns for real briefs — including a full admissions campaign for a school in India — because I'd rather learn by shipping than just studying theory.
